A COZY, PUZZLE GREEK MYTH ADVENTURE!

Set out on an enchanting, turn-based puzzle adventure as Sophia, a brave young heroine guided by the wisdom of Athena. In this cozy yet challenging journey, navigate through intricate mazes and mystical landscapes inspired by ancient Greek mythology.

NO MYSTERY IS LEFT UNSOLVED!

Embark on a gripping journey as Foxglove, an ex-detective turned private investigator with a knack for solving the toughest of cases. Immerse yourself in a captivating noir-inspired world, where shadows conceal secrets and danger lurks around every corner. As you navigate intricate crime scenes, armed with only your wits and a trusty inspection device, uncover clues, examine objects, and unravel a web of deceit. Will you uncover the truth or become another pawn in this deadly game?

~Demo currently in production~
