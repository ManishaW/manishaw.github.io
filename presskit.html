SOPHIA'S PATH PRESS KIT

Set out on an enchanting adventure as Sophia, a brave young heroine guided by the wisdom of Athena. In this cozy yet challenging journey, navigate through intricate mazes and mystical landscapes inspired by ancient Greek mythology.

GENRE: Puzzle, Adventure

PLATFORM: PC

RELEASE DATE: Coming soon

Features

  • Over 60 challenging and mythologically inspired turn-based path puzzles
  • Four more beautifully hand-drawn worlds inspired by Greek mythology
  • Meet friends who will help guide your way
  • Collect mischievous Rascals and long lost Relics along each path
  • An overworld and tracking for all path achievements
  • A heartfelt story about courage, curiosity and overcoming our fears
  • Fun Steam Achievements
  • Full gamepad support and playable on Steam Deck
  • An Original Soundtrack that brings each world to life
